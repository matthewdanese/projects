ELTA North America is a global leader in the design, manufacture and support of innovative electronic systems for the United States government. These proven high performance systems and a focus on customer satisfaction are the foundations of the company’s commitment to providing solutions to the nation’s warfighters, security personnel and first responders. The company provides solutions in the ground, maritime, airborne and security domains. ELTA is located in Fulton, MD, near Washington, DC and Baltimore, MD.
